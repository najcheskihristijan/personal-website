Why Prilep?

The Opportunity

  • ● Zero coworking spaces in Prilep (first mover)
  • ● Population: ~66,000 (4th largest city)
  • ● IT companies: IWConnect (200+ staff), GrabIT (~20 staff)
  • ● 3,100+ university students (Faculty of Economics)
  • ● ~60-70 estimated active freelancers
  • ● Prime Korzo location with foot traffic

Key Risks

  • ● Small addressable market
  • ● Rent is 53% of operating costs (ideal: 30-40%)
  • ● Declining population trend
  • ● Break-even requires near-full occupancy
  • ● Renovation investment on landlord’s property
  • ● Seasonal fluctuation (students leave in summer)

Rent Deal Analysis

Comparing Offers

MetricOwner’s OfferYour Counter-Offer
Grace Period3 months freeNone

5-Year Total€57,000€48,000
5-Year Savings—€9,000 saved
Critical: You are investing €9,000+ in renovation on the landlord’s property. Negotiate:
• Minimum 5-year lease with max 3% annual rent increase
• Right of first refusal if he sells the property
• Written clause that renovations become tenant improvements (deductible if you leave early)
Without these protections, you could renovate and lose the space.
Alternative counter: €750/month paid annually (€9,000) — matches owner’s Year 1 cost exactly, saves €3,000/year from Year 2. Worth trying before settling on €800.
